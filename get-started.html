What You Will Need

A crochet hook. Generally speaking size 14 is the best all round hook and we find bamboo is the best material to work with, though metal or plastic will work fine.

Yarn. Start of with a material such as acrylic. It is cheaper and you can easily reverse mistakes - wool is not as forgiving!

Time. Crocheting is a skill that takes practice and patience. Give yourself enough time to learn and donâ€™t be put off if things are not working for you right away. They will with practice and time.

Your own space. Find somewhere comfortable and quiet where you can relax and focus fully on learning your new skill.
